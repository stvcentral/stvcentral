# Royal Chaos shop pricing

The public shop catalogue is available at:

`/shop`

All prices are shown in Canadian dollars. Checkout is intentionally disabled until payment and fulfilment are connected.

## Approved planned prices

| Product | Planned price |
|---|---:|
| Individual song | CAD $2 |
| Individual digital card | CAD $2 |
| Digital booster | CAD $14 |
| Complete 54-card digital deck | CAD $54 |
| Individual physical card | CAD $10 + postage |
| Random 10-card physical booster | CAD $7.99 + postage |
| Custom-chosen 10-card physical booster | CAD $12.99 + postage |
| Complete 54-card physical deck | CAD $54 + postage |

Physical products include matching digital cards and songs at no additional charge. The CAD $5 difference between the random and custom booster recognizes certainty, sorting and custom packing—not a difference in the intrinsic value of the songs.

## Where pricing is stored

Edit product copy and prices in:

`data/shopProducts.js`

The public page is rendered by:

`app/shop/page.js`
